Technical Overview

Wind Whisper Bot is a voice-assisted weather assistant that combines modern conversational AI with real-time weather data from the OpenWeather API. Designed as a full-stack demo project, it showcases a wide range of skills relevant to Intelligent Virtual Assistant (IVA) and Digital Platform development.
